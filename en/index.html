Technical Due Diligence
Analysis of the technological risks of startups
Do you purchase or invest in startups?
We offer investors an analysis of the proposed technological solution, its potential and revealing all technological shortcomings and risks to startups
We add to the basic investment indicator Return on Investment (ROI)and newly an analysis of the technological risksTechnical Due Diligence (TDD)
Our clients are strategic and angel investors, Venture Capital and corporations
Analysis of the technological risks of startups
Advantages
Thanks to the analysis, you will get a real picture of the proposed technology of the startup being verified, and thus you can avoid an unsuccessful transaction or save money for investment
What you learn
  • Does the given solution correspond to the requirements of your business?
  • Is it sustainable over a long period of time?
  • What safety risks are vested in it?
  • Are the personal data and information sufficiently secured?
  • How suitable is the selected infrastructure and technology?
  • Is the solution ready for emergency utilisation and scalability?
  • Is the whole development process set up well?
Analysis content
  • Total evaluation of the project
  • Risks from the perspective of business
  • Technologies and infrastructure
  • Safety
  • Performance and scalability
  • Development process
How it works?
A startup completes a detailed on-line form
Our experts evaluate the information obtained and arrange an on-line meeting with the startup
The output is a detailed analysis of our findings which we present the analysis to the investor and propose any further steps
